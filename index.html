How to Play

Welcome to Match the Mares! Here’s how to play:

  • Click the screen to start the game.
  • Flip two cards at a time.
  • If the cards match, they stay face up.
  • If they don’t match, they flip back over.
  • Match all the cards before the time runs out.
  • Try to finish with the fewest flips possible!

About the Authors

We were inspired by memory matching games and, being huge fans of My Little Pony, we decided to create this fun, colorful game called Match the Mares!

Our goal is to make a challenging yet enjoyable game for players of all ages.

This game was developed by Keldy Lorae Patawaran, Kirsten Zoe Viray, and Sophia Mickey Rich Maquesias.

Tips & Tricks

  • Try to remember where each mare is to minimize flips.
  • Pair the cards quickly to save time!
  • Have fun and don’t worry if you lose, try again!
